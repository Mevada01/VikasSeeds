About Us

An organization committed to provide quality seeds

Ankur Seeds Pvt. Ltd. Business Built on Integrity

Pillars of Strength:

Ankur Seeds Pvt. Ltd. was founded in the year 1976 by three visionary Agriculturists Shri. Ravi Kashikar, Shri. Madhav Shembhekar, Late Shri. Laxman Aurangabadkar ably supported by Shri. Makarand Saoji, Late Shri. Vijay Kashikar, Late Shri. Balwant Umalkar.

Shri Ravi Kashikar, the Chairman rightly observes and says that “Ankur is not an organization, it is virtually a movement”. Indeed so, the pioneering work initiated and continued by this Vidarbha based company has brought prosperity to millions of farmers in many parts of country. While the founder directors continue to act as guide, the next generation has ably taken over responsibilities at technological, managerial, organizational, and institutional level to manage and control departments like, production, marketing, to research. Ankur Seeds Private Limited is poised for continued and accelerated growth and development towards sprouting and germination of seeds of prosperity for millions of farmers in the years to come. A feather in the cap was added in the form of Shri. Dilip Rodi.

Since Ankur Seeds’ inception, the focus was on R & D and exercising quality control to create a company that will be recognized by farmers across India for delivering superior hybrid seeds. This dedication has paid rich dividends as we have earned respect and trust of millions of farmers across India and beyond.

Quality Paramount:

The objective behind the venture was to provide the highest quality of seeds, adaptable to local climatic, cultivation conditions and as per specific requirements of farmers. The company today is proud to have developed over 200 varieties in 16 crops, and is one of the leading seed producers in India having an annual turnover of 5 billion.

By keeping in mind intense requirement of nutrition by high yielding seeds and better crop health, Dharti Agro Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. was founded in the year 1998.

Production and Processing:

With 11 State-of-the-art processing plants, our total production capacity is over 16000 tons; dehumidified storage capacity of 5000 sq meter for medium terms storage & more than 10,000 sq. meter warehousing facility. Our plants can perform drying, pre-cleaning, ginning, delinting, gravity separation and fungicidal treatments. Our breeding support center (R&D Station) meets the norms and infrastructure guidelines set by the DBT, Govt. of India.

Ankur has never failed to acknowledge the tremendous contribution made by the seed growing farmers, who have given unstinted support to become better. While initially there were around 1000 farmers associated with Ankur, currently more than 100,000 farmers are producing seeds for us.

Ankur Seeds Private Limited

In the last 44 years Ankur Seeds Pvt.Ltd has become a widely recognized and respected company in the seed Industry. It is India’s one of the premier seed organization offering a wide range of seeds in cotton, field crops and vegetables to the farmers. We are having a network in almost all the states of the country.

Our Journey

  • YEAR 1976 - FOUNDATION YEAR

    A seed of friendship was sown by the visionary agriculturists to nurture their dreams with sheer determination to succeed with a modest office of 200 sq ft area in Central India, Nagpur.

  • YEAR 1984 - TURNING POINT

    This was the year when Ankur Agro Seeds and Chemicals, a partnership firm became a Pvt Ltd Company.

  • YEAR 1985 - CORE EXPANSION

    Major expansion - Regional Marketing Offices & Processing Plants were set up across India.

  • YEAR 1985 - FIRST HYBRID

    Proud to be the first Indian company to launch hybrid technology in Okra(bhendi), Okra AROH-1 and Okra 35.

  • YEAR 1993 - RECOGNITION OF EFFORTS

    Research and Development Division of Ankur Seeds received recognition from DSIR, Government of India.

  • YEAR 1994 - MEGA FIELD DAYS

    Pioneers in conducting mega field days where thousands of farmers visited the demo plots.

  • YEAR 1995 to 2005 - 651 HYBRID ERA

    The early and drought tolerant hybrid which brought laurels to the company.

  • YEAR 1996 - AWARD WINNING MOMENT

    Ankur Seeds received National Award for Excellent Research and Development achievements from Honorable Minister of State for Science and Technology.

  • YEAR 1998 - MARKET LEADER

    Ankur started marketing of research based Rice varieties like Sonam which still rule the market.

  • YEAR 1998 - DHARTI TAKES SHAPE

    Dharti, a Seed and Plant Health Company an extended arm of Ankur Seed was established and was envisioned with activities of manufacturing and formulating various Macro-nutrients and Micro-nutrients based products.

  • YEAR 2002 - WELCOME CHILLI

    Chilli 228 was well accepted in Guntur market.

  • YEAR 2003 - BT COTTON BOOM

    Ankur Seeds was amongst the first three seed companies adopted the genetically modified technology for cotton, popularly known as 'Bt-cotton' and there was no looking back.

  • YEAR 2004 - BREAKTHROUGH

    Turnover crossed the 100 Crore landmark.

  • YEAR 2008 - KINHI DEMO

    Ankur continues to hold Farmers Meet and do Field Visits at the center.

  • YEAR 2012 - MILESTONE YEAR

    Turnover crossed the 500 Crore landmark.

  • YEAR 2012 - R & D EXPANSION

    Inauguration of world class breeding support centre, to constantly innovate and to keep pace with the modern technologies.

  • YEAR 2014 - CORPORATE EXCELLENCE AWARD

    Lokmat awarded Ankur Seeds for its outstanding services.

  • YEAR 2015 - GROWING HANDS

    Total 852 employees and still growing.

  • YEAR 2017 - HYBRID COWPEA LAUNCH

    Ankur Seeds was the first company in the world to give GMS based Cowpea Hybrids.

  • YEAR 2018 to 2020 - Advancement

    Acquisition of talent and upgradation of facility to incorporate modern technologies like genomic selection, digitalization of data recording, automated phenotyping etc.

  • Year 2019 - BRAND LEADERSHIP AWARD

    World Marketing Congress awarded Ankur Seeds for Brand Leadership.

Ravi Kashikar (Chairman)

It's because of people like Shri. Ravi Kashikar, the founder of Ankur Seeds Pvt. Ltd that improbable dreams are attained. With his hand-picked pool of talent he’s made Ankur Seeds Private Ltd from being a local brand to a highly recognized one in the farming sector across India. Ravi Kashikar till date remains as involved & dedicated to his work; positively impacting lives of farmer brethren. He indirectly not only provides livelihood to the farmers associated with Ankur Seeds, but also makes sure that he connects with them emotionally and understands their needs.

Madhav Shembekar (Managing Director)

Madhav Shembekar brings exceptional management skills to the table; motivating & driving employees to do and give their best. He helps his personnel to deal with issues related to farmer connect, nature connect, innovations, climate etc. equipping and empowering them at every step to deal with situations. Collaboration and empathy are his guiding principles through which he builds his team, inspiring them to work in tandem.

Late Laxman Aurangabadkar (Former Research Director)

He was the founder Director who oversaw company’s Research and Development department. He had the vision to develop and adopt new technologies to meet the ever changing demands of the famers and provide research based products adaptable to the varied agro-climatic zones of the country. He set the organization on the path of always being future-ready!

 

Makarand Saoji (Marketing Director)

He heads marketing vertical of the company. He aims to create brand equity and constantly evaluates innovation process. He focuses to deliver finest quality seeds across the country and furthermore also earn the trust and build healthy relationship with millions of farmers, dealer and distribution channel. His strategies helped the organization touch newer heights.

Late Vijay Kashikar (Former Production Director)

One of the founder directors, his contribution in building a network between seed growers and mediators and ensuring that the company acquired proper produce from the contact farmers remains relevant till date. He laid the groundwork for making Ankur Seeds the symbol of trust; never compromised on the quality of seeds and made only the best processed seeds available for marketing.

Late Balwant Umalkar (Former Finance Director)

He was an acute and analytical Finance man who helped the organization to set its systems in order that till date drive our financial operations smoothly. His keen observation and cost saving attitude was an asset to the company.

Dilip Rodi (Financial Advisor)

He looks after activities that impact Ankur Seed’s financial performance. He is responsible for financial planning, developing and implementing accounting policies, tax planning and audits, preparing forecasts and budgets among other functions. A strategic thinker with exceptional numerical proficiency, the organization went on to become a financially stable company under his supervision and leadership.
